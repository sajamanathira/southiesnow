Level 88 - Condition Names
WORKING-STORAGE SECTION.
01  WS-STATUS  PIC X(01).
PROCEDURE DIVISION.
    IF WS-STATUS = 'S'
    PERFORM PARA-SINGLE
    ELSE
    IF WS-STATUS = 'M'
       PERFORM PARA-MARRIED
    ELSE
       PERFORM PARA-OTHER
        END-IF
    END-IF

01  WS-ZONE PIC X(05).
PROCEDURE DIVISION.
    EVALUATE WS-ZONE
    WHEN 'NORTH'
       PERFORM PARA-NORTH
    WHEN 'SOUTH'

    WHEN OTHER
       PERFORM PARA-OTHER
    END-EVALUATE
